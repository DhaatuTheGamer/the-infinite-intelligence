# Product Definition: The Infinite Intelligence

## Vision
To transcend the limitations of single-model AI responses by simulating a collaborative, multi-perspective "council of experts" that debates, critiques, and synthesizes complex prompts into high-fidelity intelligence.

## Core Problem
Traditional AI outputs often suffer from single-viewpoint bias, lack of critical self-review, and a failure to break down complex problems into first principles.

## Solution
An orchestrator that:
1.  **Decomposes**: Breaks prompts into First Principles.
2.  **Assembles**: Recruits specialized agent personas (Logos, Pathos, Ethos, Praxis).
3.  **Collaborates**: Facilitates parallel or sequential agent interaction.
4.  **Critiques**: Runs multi-round peer-review cycles.
5.  **Synthesizes**: Merges outputs into a cohesive, actionable final report.

## Target Audience
- Strategic planners and decision-makers.
- Developers and architects solving complex system problems.
- Creative writers and researchers looking for nuanced perspectives.

## Success Metrics
- **Accuracy**: Reduced hallucinations via peer critique.
- **Depth**: Multi-faceted answers covering logic, ethics, and actionability.
- **UX**: Seamless visualization of the "thinking" process.
